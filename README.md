- CityBlend Sales Analysis (Excel)

- Overview
This project analyzes six months of sales data (January–June) from "CityBlend", a coffee shop chain based in New York with three locations.
The goal is to understand overall business performance and identify key sales trends.

- Tools Used
  Microsoft Excel
  Pivot Tables
  Formulas

  Key Insights and business recommendations
- Data Quality & Coverage
A total of 149,116 transactions were recorded between January and June.
However, missing transactions were detected, indicating potential data inconsistencies.
This suggests a need to validate data completeness with the operations team to ensure accurate reporting.

- Revenue Trends
Revenue experienced a 6.77% decrease from January to February (-$5,532.55).
From February onwards, revenue showed a consistent upward trend.
Overall, revenue increased by 103.83% from January to June, indicating strong business growth despite early fluctuations.

-Product Category Performance
The Coffee category dominates, accounting for 39.17% of total transactions, making it the core driver of sales.
Lower-performing categories such as Packaged Chocolate and Branded products may indicate:
Low customer demand
Or potential stock availability issues
This highlights an opportunity to optimize inventory and evaluate product strategy.

-Store Performance
The Hell's Kitchen location generated the highest revenue, with a total of $236,511.17 over the six-month period.
This suggests stronger demand or better performance compared to other locations.

Barista Espresso is the top-performing product:
16,403 units sold
$91,406.20 in revenue
This product represents a key revenue driver and could be leveraged for promotions or upselling strategies.
